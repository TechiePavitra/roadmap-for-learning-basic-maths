# 📘 Mastering Basic Mathematics – Beginner Roadmap

This roadmap guides **complete beginners** through fundamental math topics step by step.  
It’s designed for **self-study, daily revision, or classroom support**.

---

## 🛤️ Roadmap & Study Plan

### 1️⃣ Numbers & Operations (Week 1–2)

**Topics**
- Natural Numbers, Whole Numbers, Integers
- Negative Numbers, Absolute Value
- Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division
- Order of Operations (PEMDAS / BODMAS)
- Factors, Multiples, Prime Numbers
- LCM & GCF

**Real-life examples**
- Counting money, temperatures, shopping bills

**Milestone**
✔ Can add, subtract, multiply, and divide whole numbers confidently  
✔ Can find factors, multiples, LCM, and GCF

---

### 2️⃣ Fractions, Decimals & Percentages (Week 3–4)

**Topics**
- Fractions: proper, improper, mixed, simplification
- Equivalent & Comparing Fractions
- Operations with Fractions (+ – × ÷)
- Decimals: place value, conversion with fractions, operations
- Percentages: conversion between %, fractions, decimals
- Percentage of a number, increase & decrease

**Real-life examples**
- Fractions: cooking recipes  
- Decimals: money & measurements  
- Percentages: discounts, interest rates

**Milestone**
✔ Can solve word problems using fractions, decimals, and percentages

---

### 3️⃣ Powers & Roots (Week 5)

**Topics**
- Exponents: meaning, laws of exponents, negative exponents
- Roots: square roots, cube roots

**Real-life examples**
- Exponents: area (cm²), volume (cm³)  
- Roots: finding side lengths from area of a square

**Milestone**
✔ Can simplify and calculate with exponents and roots

---

### 4️⃣ Algebra Basics (Week 6–7)

**Topics**
- What is a variable?
- Expressions vs. Equations
- Solving one-step and two-step equations
- Balancing method
- Word problems with simple algebra

**Real-life examples**
- Solving for unknowns in expenses (e.g., “If 3 apples cost $6, how much is 1 apple?”)

**Milestone**
✔ Can form and solve simple equations with one unknown

---

### 5️⃣ Geometry & Measurement (Week 8–9)

**Topics**
- Lines & Angles: types, complementary/supplementary, parallel/perpendicular
- Triangles: types, properties, angle sums
- Quadrilaterals & Polygons
- Circles: radius, diameter, circumference, area
- 3D Shapes: cube, cuboid, sphere, cylinder, cone
- Surface area & volume

**Real-life examples**
- Measuring land, designing objects, construction, packaging

**Milestone**
✔ Can calculate perimeter, area, and volume of basic shapes

---

### 6️⃣ Data & Probability (Week 10)

**Topics**
- Collecting data
- Pictographs, bar graphs, line graphs
- Mean, Median, Mode
- Probability basics: dice, coins, cards

**Real-life examples**
- Sports statistics, weather forecasts, game outcomes

**Milestone**
✔ Can calculate averages and simple probabilities

---

## ⏳ Study Routine

- 📖 **Daily**: Study one concept (20–30 minutes)  
- 📝 **Practice**: Solve 5–10 problems daily  
- 🔁 **Weekly Review**: Go back and revise weak areas  
- 🎯 **Checkpoint**: Move to the next topic **only after reaching the milestone**

---

## ✅ Final Goal

After completing this roadmap (≈ 10 weeks), you will:  
- Be confident with numbers, fractions, percentages, and basic algebra  
- Understand geometry and measurement for everyday use  
- Read and interpret basic statistics and probability  
- Be ready to move on to **Pre-Algebra / High School Math**
